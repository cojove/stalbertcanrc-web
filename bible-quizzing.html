BIBLE QUIZZING

Welcome to St. Albert Bible Quizzing!

Bible Quizzing is a Bible memorization program that has church teams from across Alberta memorizing God's Word in a friendly yet competitive way.

Quizzing Material

This year we are studying and memorizing from 1 and 2 Corinthians, using the NIV 2011 translation.

There is an eight year rotation as follows: 1 & 2 Corinthians; John; Hebrews/1&2 Peter; Matthew; Romans/James; Acts; Galatians/Ephesians/Philippians/Colossians; Luke.

Quizzers may choose how many verses to memorize.

The District we compete in officially recognizes the following Clubs:

  • Club 50: memorize 50 assigned verses.
  • Club 100: memorize 100 assigned verses.
  • Club 150: memorize 150 assigned verses.
  • Club 300: memorize 300 assigned verses.
  • Club Text: memorize the entire text.

No matter how many verses quizzers memorize, quizzers are encouraged to read and study all the material to understand the context of the verses they memorize.

Quizzing Schedule

The Bible Quizzing schedule consists of:

Weekly Quiz Practices

Practices usually include a Bible study on that week's material, memory verse quoting, practice quizzes and some time to socialize.

  • Thursdays from 7:00pm to 9:00pm at our Church building (54203 Range Road 261, St. Albert, AB).
  • First practice scheduled for September 4, 2025 (no memorization required)
Quiz Meets

At Quiz Meets, teams from different churches compete to answer questions based on the material studied thus far.

  • Oct 17-18: Regional Meet (Bon Accord)
  • Nov 21-22: District #1 (Meadlowlands Baptist, Edmonton)
  • Jan 30-31: District #2/Zone (West Edmonton)
  • Mar 6-7 or Mar 13-14: District #3 (Calgary)
  • Apr 24-26: District Finals (Camp Nakamun)
Great West Invitational

The top 25 quizzers from our district are invited to compete against the top quizzers from four other western North American districts.

  • Mar 28: Great West Practice (Location TBD)
  • Apr 9-12: Great West Quiz Meet (Location TBD)

Quizzing Expectations

Below are some expectations of quizzers:

  • Attend practices and arrive ready to start at 7:00pm. Please tell a coach if you are unable to attend or will be late.
  • Bring your quiz book and quote sheet to each practice and be prepared to quote the assigned memory verses for that week.
  • Be a team player: support, encourage and pray for each other.

In order to make Bible Quizzing a successful activity, parents are strongly encouraged to be involved. Some ways to be involved include:

  • participate in the weekly Bible study
  • listen to quizzers quote verses they have memorized
  • coach a team at a quiz meet
  • learn table staff positions: Quiz Master, Answer Judge, Score Keeper
