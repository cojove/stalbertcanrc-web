About God

"We all believe with the heart and confess with the mouth that there is only one God...; he is eternal, incomprehensible, invisible, immutable, infinite, almighty, perfectly wise, just, good, and the overflowing fountain of all good."

(Belgic Confession, Article 1)

God reveals himself to us through the creation and government of the universe and through his holy and divine Word as contained in the Holy Bible. Learn more about God by reading the Bible and studying sermons.

Online Bible Sermons

"Your word is a lamp to my feet and a light to my path."

(Psalm 119: 105)

The life changing message contained in the Holy Bible about God, and his son Jesus, may be outlined in a few key points...

God created the world. He made us rulers of the world under him.
We disobey and reject God, our ruler, and try to run life our own way.
God is just and won't let our rebellion go unpunished. God's punishment for rebellion is death.
God showed his love and mercy by sending his Son into the world: Jesus Christ. Jesus obeyed God perfectly, died in our place and brought forgiveness.
God raised Jesus to life again and he ascended into heaven and sits at the right hand of God. Jesus has conquered death, gives new life, and will return to judge.
We need to have faith in Jesus and submit to his rule in order to receive forgiveness and eternal life.

"For God did not appoint us to suffer wrath but to receive salvation through our Lord Jesus Christ. He died for us so that, whether we are awake or asleep, we may live together with him. Therefore encourage one another and build each other up, just as in fact you are doing."

(1 Thessalonians 5: 9-11)

At our church you can expect to hear the faithful preaching of the Bible and to enjoy encouragement and support from fellow believers. Going to church provides the opportunity to praise and worship God, our Creator and Saviour.

In addition to church services on Sunday, there are many ways to be involved in church life: Bible studies, youth groups, committees, special events, service projects, etc.

Our church was instituted in 2003. We are a member of the Canadian and American Reformed Churches. Visit our federation's home page for more information on what we believe as a church.
